No, we don't offer payment on delivery at the moment. However, pick-up options are available for customers who prefer to pay in person.
How long do your perfumes last?
Our perfumes are long-lasting, with an average projection of 8–12 hours, depending on the perfume type and your skin chemistry.
Do you deliver outside Lagos?
Yes, we deliver nationwide across Nigeria. Delivery within Lagos is same-day or next-day, while outside Lagos takes 3–5 working days.
